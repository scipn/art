About Me

Bonjour!

Je m’appelle Isabelle Lucienne Eugene.

I’m a multifaceted creative professional with a love for art, fashion, writing, film and all things digital media. I have a background in content creation, creative direction, writing, editing, social media, video production, photography, illustration, and graphic design.

I hope to inspire and create a more beautiful, sustainable and eco-innovative future for others with my skills.

I developed a strong interest in fashion and digital media in high school while modeling in my local non-profit art museum’s recycled fashion shows and while freelancing on the digital marketplace known as Wanelo. My account was listed on the Top Women’s Page and I eventually gained over 140K followers as an e-commerce influencer.

Since then, I graduated from The Zimmerman School of Advertising and Mass Communications department of Multimedia Journalism and Production at the University of South Florida. I also pursued a minor from The School of Interdisciplinary Global Studies in International Studies and a certificate of Visualization & Design from the USF School of Art & Art History.

During those three years I established roles as an intern at a luxury footwear label of Vogue, a freelance writer at a digital marketing agency, a lifestyle editor for the award-winning campus newspaper and online platform, a senior editor of an online magazine for college women, and as a digital media intern of an organization dedicated to the empowerment of women in international trade.

When I’m not freelancing I’m fond of painting, reading, taking photos, being in nature, visiting museums, gallery hopping, going to film festivals, traveling, and learning about sustainability.

If you would like to learn more about me, please feel free to reach out.

Merci!
